The focus of conventional education delivery is on providing an individual with knowledge and some skills. These skills and knowledge are deemed essential for an individual to make a career for himself in today’s competitive world.

This view, which is anything but narrow, doesn’t help the individual identify himself from within and grow to his true potential and live with the world in harmony.

There is a need to look at learning from a broader perspective and create a more wholesome learning environment.

Samvida is a unique experiment in enabling learning for children. The primary stakeholders in this community are the children and their parents. Parents at Samvida are not consumers of a service, but are partners in the endeavour. Hence, we are keen on building lasting parent partnerships. By this, we mean parents who by choice want to provide an alternate learning environment for their and will play an active role in the operations and teaching learning processes. In fact, several learning facilitators at Samvida are parents. A parent can choose any area to make significant contribution.

Currently, we are welcoming partnerships from parents whose children are between ages 6 and 12. The Samvida community will be delighted to have on board enthusiastic experts, from all fields. We welcome participation by passionate people from different areas, so that our children have the opportunity to interact and grow with their support.
